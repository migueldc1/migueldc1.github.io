About me

Hello! I'm Miguel de Celis, a postdoctoral researcher focused on unlocking the potential of microbiomes in both natural and artificial ecosystems.

I am a passionate researcher in the field of microbial ecology, specialising in bioinformatic analysis. My work focuses on exploring microbial communities in diverse environments, including wine fermentation, wastewater treatment, agricultural soils, and plants. By integrating classical microbiological techniques with bioinformatics and ecological insights, I aim to harness the potential of these microbial communities to enhance key biological processes.

I am always open to start new collaborations in any research topic, specially regarding microbial ecology and microbial association networks.
